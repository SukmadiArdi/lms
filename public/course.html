Understanding Visual Hierarchy

Master the art of guiding the user's eye through strategic placement, sizing, and contrast.

In this lesson, we explore the fundamental principles of visual hierarchy and how it directly impacts user engagement and comprehension. You'll learn to manipulate size, color, contrast, and spacing to create intuitive and scannable interfaces.

Key Learning Objectives

  • check The Role of Scale: Understand how establishing dominance through proportional sizing guides the user journey.
  • check Color Weight & Contrast: Utilize deep primary colors against neutral backgrounds to draw attention to critical actions.
  • check Whitespace as an Element: Learn why negative space is crucial for grouping related content and reducing cognitive load.

Pro Tip for Designers

Before adding more color to highlight an element, try increasing the whitespace around it first. Often, isolation is stronger than decoration.
